# Privacy Policy for Uplift Forge

**Last Updated:** February 27, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Uplift Forge ("the Application") is a desktop application designed to process and analyze engineering performance data by connecting to your Atlassian JIRA instance. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal data is handled in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

## 2. Data We Collect and Process
The Application operates entirely locally on your device. We do not operate any centralized servers to collect, store, or process your personal data. 

To function, the Application processes the following data:
- **Authentication Data:** Your JIRA Base URL, Email Address, and Atlassian API Token.
- **JIRA Metadata:** JIRA issue summaries, descriptions, statuses, transition histories, and custom fields.
- **Personal Data of JIRA Users:** Names, Account IDs, and Avatars of team members associated with the JIRA instance.

## 3. How Data is Stored
All data collected by the Application is stored **locally on your device**.
- Authentication credentials (Email, API Token) are encrypted using your operating system's native secure storage mechanism (e.g., macOS Keychain, Windows Credential Manager).
- JIRA metadata and user information are cached locally in application data directories to improve performance and allow offline viewing.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The Application communicates directly with the following third-party services:
- **Atlassian JIRA:** To fetch issues, statuses, fields, and user data based on the provided credentials.
- **GitHub:** To check for application updates. This process may expose your IP address to GitHub as part of a standard HTTP request.

**No data is sent to the developer of Uplift Forge.**

## 5. Your Rights Under GDPR
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can exercise your GDPR rights as follows:
- **Right to Access/Portability:** You can view all processed data within the Application.
- **Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** You can delete all your data by using the "Reset App" feature within the Application or by manually deleting the Application's data folder on your system.
- **Right to Rectification:** You can update your credentials or refresh your JIRA data at any time.

## 6. Consent
By using Uplift Forge, you consent to the local processing and storage of the aforementioned data on your device, and to the transmission of this data directly to your configured Atlassian JIRA instance.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any significant changes via the Application's update mechanism.
